Speculative design

Olfactory self-tracking

Track the smells in your life for later retrieval.

A project with
 Julia Brandt, Anna Wahl

What we did
 problem framing, concept development, interaction design, digital prototyping, visual design

Brief

For this two-day project, we were asked to come up with a concept and a prototype supporting or challenging the notion of self-tracking. It was up to us to determine what data we envision tracking, and how we choose to represent the data.

Smells as data

We saw the exercise as an opportunity to speculate about olfactory data as the next – in a growing range of personal and bodily measurements – to be tracked, analysed, and shared. What do the smells in your life say about you? The ones you encounter, and the ones you give off yourself? What if you could not only log those smells, but also sniff them later?
